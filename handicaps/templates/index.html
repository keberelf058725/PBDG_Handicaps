About Us

Palm Beach Disc Golf will facilitate the continued growth and development of Palm Beach County disc golf courses and oversee club functions. It is our desire to work together with the county, club members, disc golfers throughout southern Florida, and the general public to support and promote both the club and the sport of disc golf as a whole. We will seek to accomplish this through the hosting of weekly leagues, PDGA sanctioned events, club fundraisers, clinics, and other tournaments. In addition to the promotion of our sport and club, Palm Beach Disc Golf will work with the county parks department and local businesses in whatever way possible in order to maintain a positive and viable influence on the community. We embrace the qualities of integrity, good sportsmanship, and friendly competitiveness. We will strive to be the best disc golf club in South Florida.
